Amazon Sales Dashboard

This project presents an end-to-end analysis of Amazon sales data, aimed at uncovering key business insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer demand, regional trends, and operational efficiency. The dashboard enables stakeholders to make data-driven decisions by visualizing trends across time, geography, product categories, and shipping performance.

Key Insights:
• Total Sales: 3.59M with Profit of 108.42K
• Peak Sales Year (2014): 1.27M
• Highest Sales Month: December (0.64M)
• Top Performing City: Los Angeles (0.87M)
• Best-Selling Categories: Tables and Phones

These insights highlight strong regional performance, seasonal demand, and opportunities for logistics optimization.

Technologies: Power BI, Python, SQL, Pandas

Electric Vehicle Adoption

This Electric Vehicle Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of electric vehicle (EV) adoption across different cities, states, vehicle makes, and models. It highlights the growth of EVs over time, evaluates range performance, and assesses eligibility for clean energy incentives.

Key Insights:
• Total Electric Vehicles Tracked: 150,480
• Average Electric Range: 67.88 miles
• Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV): 117K (78%)
• Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV): 34K (22%)

These insights indicate a strong preference for full battery-electric vehicles over plug-in hybrids.
